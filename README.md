## Quality

Quality is a first principle in every system I touch.

I care about code that reads clearly, behaves predictably, survives change, and keeps its shape under pressure. That means strong architecture, explicit boundaries, careful naming, useful tests, observability, and the discipline to hold the bar high when speed is tempting shortcuts.

For me, quality is inseparable from trust. Teams trust systems that behave well. Users trust products that feel deliberate. Companies trust codebases that remain understandable as they grow.
